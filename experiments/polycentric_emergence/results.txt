  complexity lens  : endogenous = near-lumpable modular structure matched to sub-

                     atomized = collapsed basin.
  active-inference : quota = institution's policy prior; monocentric = one over-precise
                     central prior (high control, no responsiveness = calcification);
                     endogenous = nested per-niche priors that track local conditions.
  => a known MAS result (emergent/decentralized beats imposed top-down) recovered AND
     mechanistically explained. EI certifies a controller exists; FIT certifies its
     setpoint serves the governed. Polycentric co-maintains both.
